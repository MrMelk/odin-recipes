Fridtjof Steak

Cooking the perfect Fridtjof

Contrary to popular belief Fridtjof is best coocked spitroasted over a hot grill. This allows for the perfect medium rare consistency and juicyness

You will need

  • 1 whole Fridtjof
  • 1 roaring fire or hot grill
  • 1 2 meter metal rod

Approach

  1. Stab Fridtjof vertically with pole
  2. Mount it to grill or bonfire
  3. Slowly rotate over the course of 2-3 hours
  4. Enjoy

And now you have your Fridtjof steak
